About Us

Our college offers a wide range of rigorous academic programs in the fields of business and information technology that are designed to prepare students to thrive in a rapidly changing global marketplace. we provide our students with hands-on learning opportunities, innovative teaching methods, and cutting-edge technologies, all of which enable them to gain the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in their chosen careers.

Our Mission

To provide a transformative education that equips students with the skills, knowledge, and ethical framework to succeed in the fields of business and information technology.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution of higher education in business and IT, driving innovation and entrepreneurship, and preparing students to become successful professionals and responsible leaders who make a positive difference in the world.

EXAM BODIES

We have affliated with several Exam bodies to make our courses remain relevant in the dynamic job market.

KNEC

The agency is committed to ensuring the integrity and fairness of the examination process and promoting quality education in Kenya.

KASNEB

It is responsible for conducting professional and diploma examinations in accounting, finance, management, and governance, as well as certification and registration of professionals in these fields.

ICM

UK-based professional body and examination board that offers a range of qualifications in business, management, and related fields.

JP-UK

JP-UK qualifications are recognized by employers, universities, and professional bodies in the UK and abroad, and they are designed to meet the needs of learners at all levels of education and experience.

NITA

NITA (National Industrial Training Authority) is a Kenyan government agency that is responsible for regulating, coordinating, and promoting industrial training in the country.
